Location



Romania is today a full member of the European Union and will have a share in the growth and development of the EU economy. Romania attracts investors in activities in which possesses comparative advantages, thus accelerating the transformation of Romania into a regional centre and an exclusive tourism destination.


The land of 32.000 sq.m. is situated in Ciocanu, Brasov wider area, a unique area very much appreciated, as a deluxe tourist area. Ciocanu is a location in Dambovicioara, Arges county, Muntenia, Romania. Ciocanu village looks like a bucket surrounded by the Bucegi Mountains to the east, Iezer-Papusa and Piatra Craiului Mountains to the west. The village is located at the historical limit between Wallachia and Transylvania, at an altitude of 1240 m above sea level. Almost half of the commune territory lies in the “Piatra Craiului” National Park.

Piatra Craului is an emerging Alpinist centre for climbers. Brashov and Predeal which are in close range are famous ski resorts and the Bran Dracula Castle is also within the area making this an interesting place for vacation villas for developers.


The location of the land is exceptional, in terms of its shape, gentle slope, and wonderful view. The land has amphitheatrical shape which offers a fantastic view of the Piatra Craului mountain from any position within. The land is clear with a trees found on in its edges and is adjacent to a natural forest.
